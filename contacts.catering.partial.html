Catering & Banquets

All of our restaurants in New York, Boston, and Chicago offer catering and banquets for our clients.

We can create custom menus to suit your needs and budget, and offer a variety of setup and presentation options. You can also book our private banquet room for larger events. Please contact us for more information.

  • Booking Cancellation

    Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours in advance. Please call us to cancel your reservation.

  • Booking Finalization

    All reservations must be finalised with the restaurant no later than 12 hours prior to the reservation date.

  • Minimum Spend

    There is no minimum spend required for a reservation. The exception is group bookings for 6+ people.

Important Notice

We are taking extra precautions in our catering services. This includes using disposable plates and utensils, etc.
